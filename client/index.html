    How You Can Get Involved:

    Sponsor one of our animals

    With your donation towards an animal of your choosing, you can help to keep our animals happy. This package offers you:

  • A signed certificate of verification.
  • Access to our open-day events for no additional cost, where you can meet your new friend.
  • Additional cuddly toy of your animal and gift bag

    • Cost: £5 a month (+ £3 one-off payment for the gift bag)

    Attend an open day

    We regularly open up our farm to members of the public, when we have activities taking place for the whole family!

    Some of the events include:

  • Petting zoo
  • Pony rides
  • Food and drink stalls

    • Our next open day is Saturday 18th March, 10am - 5pm

    Cost: £8 per child, £3 per adult

    Volunteer on the farm

    We're always looking for more animal-lovers to join our team, so why not sign yourself up?

    Starting from just 3 hours of volunteering time per month, we offer flexibilty to allow our volunteers the chance to get involved at a time that suits them.

    All of our volunteers get free access to open days and 2x gift vouchers for family and friends to use.
